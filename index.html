Deadlift (trapbar)

Instructions

  1. We start off by setting our feet shoulder width apart inside the trapbar, and lean forward with your upper body until you reach the handles.
  2. Grab the handles and try to push your chest upwards towards the ceiling and simultaneously lower your hips until your shins are no longer vertically aligned.
  3. Stand up by pushing your hips forward until you’re standing upright.

Tip: Keep your chin down, think of it like you're supposed to have a double chin during the whole exercise.

Squat (highbar)

Instructions

  1. Place the bar on safely on your neck, the most common place to rest the bar is just below th C7 vertebrae. Your feet should be approximately shoulder width apart.
  2. After you've found a good position start the movement by gentle and slowly pushing your hips backwards and downwards, while also keeping your knees from caving inwards.
  3. Keep going downwards as long as you can keep a neutral/straight upper body.
  4. When at your lowest position, turn the movement and pushing upward until you reach your starting position.

Tip: Before starting every new repetition, fill your lungs with as much air as possible creating a ballon function around your core. This techniques ensures a safer but also a stronger stance at the lowest point.
